Good to know

Frequently asked questions

How much does a WOF cost at Swanson Worx?
A Warrant of Fitness inspection is $50 to $70 and is usually done while you wait. If anything fails, we explain it in plain English and quote any repair before we start work.
Do I need to book, or can I walk in?
Walk-ins are welcome for a WOF, but booking is best for servicing and repairs. You can get an instant estimate and book online above, or call us on 09-833 9988.
Where are you located?
We're at 720D Swanson Road, Swanson, West Auckland 0612 — easy off-street parking, close to Swanson train station.
What areas do you serve?
We look after drivers across West Auckland including Swanson, Ranui, Henderson, Massey, Te Atatū, Glen Eden, Waitākere and Kumeū.
How long does a car service take?
Most services are completed the same day, and many within a few hours. We aim to turn the majority of jobs around in a single day.
Do you supply tyres and do wheel alignments?
Yes — we supply and fit major tyre brands, balance wheels and carry out laser wheel alignments. Swanson Worx is a Dynamic Wheel Co dealer.
